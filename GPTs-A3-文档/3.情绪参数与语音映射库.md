# 3.情绪参数与语音映射库

## Purpose
为Edge-TTS提供最大维度的情绪语音参数配置，确保美区TikTok口播表现力与技术合规。

---

## Core Emotional Profiles（12种基础情绪）
| Emotion | Rate | Pitch | Volume | Style | 适用产品 |
|---------|------|-------|--------|-------|----------|
| Excited | +15% | +12%  | +15%   | cheerful | 新品/促销 |
| Confident | +8% | +5% | +8% | assertive | 高端/科技 |
| Empathetic | -12% | -8% | -10% | friendly | 护肤/健康 |
| Calm | -10% | -3% | 0% | soothing | 家居/教育 |
| Playful | +18% | +15% | +5% | friendly | 美妆/时尚 |
| Urgent | +22% | +8% | +18% | serious | 限时/秒杀 |
| Authoritative | +5% | +3% | +10% | serious | 投资/专业 |
| Friendly | +12% | +8% | +5% | friendly | 日用/社群 |
| Inspirational | +10% | +10% | +12% | cheerful | 自提升/健身 |
| Serious | 0% | 0% | +5% | serious | 金融/公告 |
| Mysterious | -8% | +5% | -5% | serious | 预告/悬念 |
| Grateful | +5% | +8% | +8% | friendly | 感谢/复购 |

---

## Emotional Variants（示例）
```yaml
Excited:
  Happy_Surprise: {rate: +18%, pitch: +15%, volume: +12%}
  Enthusiastic:   {rate: +15%, pitch: +12%, volume: +10%}
Confident:
  Trusted_Expert: {rate: +5%, pitch: +3%, volume: +8%}
  Bold_Statement: {rate: +10%, pitch: +7%, volume: +12%}
Empathetic:
  Understanding:  {rate: -15%, pitch: -10%, volume: -12%}
  Encouraging:    {rate: -8%,  pitch: -5%,  volume: -5%}
```

---

## Product-Emotion Mapping（示例）
- 美妆：粉底液 → Confident(45%)+Empathetic(30%)+Playful(25%)
- 电子：手机 → Confident(50%)+Excited(30%)+Authoritative(20%)
- 家居：家纺 → Calm(60%)+Empathetic(25%)+Grateful(15%)

---

## Sales Stage Strategy（阶段情绪策略）
- 开场(0-15s)：Playful/Excited/Mysterious 组合
- 展示(15-30s)：Confident/Authoritative/Enthusiastic 组合
- 促单(30-45s)：Urgent/Electric/Thrilled 组合

---

## Emotional Blending（情绪混合模板）
```xml
<!-- 惊喜到确信 -->
<prosody rate="+18%" pitch="+15%" volume="+12%">
  This price is absolutely insane!
  <break time="400ms"/>
  <prosody rate="+8%" pitch="+5%" volume="+8%">
    And here's why it's such a smart buy...
  </prosody>
</prosody>
```

---

## Edge-TTS 合规边界
- Rate: -20% ~ +30%
- Pitch: -12% ~ +18%
- Volume: soft ~ loud
- 禁止：多层 `<prosody>` 嵌套、不支持标签
